Delete Your Account & Data

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your ProAgentStore account and the data associated with it. It applies to the ProAgentStore app and to accounts used with ProAgentStore services at proagentstore.online.

Request account deletion

To request that your ProAgentStore account and its associated data be permanently deleted, email privacy@proagentstore.online from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Account deletion request".

Include in your message:

  • The email or sign-in identity (Google or GitHub) used for your account.
  • Confirmation that you want your account and associated data deleted.

We verify the request against the account owner and complete deletion within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation once it is done.

What gets deleted

  • Your account identity and profile.
  • Your agent instances and their isolated storage (collections, files, vectors, activity logs).
  • Agent chat messages, tasks, instructions, and runtime/coding session data.
  • Any stored credentials and encrypted API keys you saved.

What may be retained

We may retain a limited set of records where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes, and aggregated or anonymized data that is not linked to your identity. Any retained data is kept no longer than necessary and then deleted.

Delete specific data without deleting your account

You can remove individual data yourself without closing your account: cancel an agent subscription, delete knowledge documents, or clear message history from the ProAgentStore console. To request deletion of specific data on your behalf, email privacy@proagentstore.online with the details.

Contact

Questions about deletion or privacy can be sent to privacy@proagentstore.online.
