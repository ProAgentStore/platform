ProAgentStore

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Overview

ProAgentStore ("PAGS", "we", "our", or "the app") is a native app for operating private ProAgentStore AI agents. This policy explains what the iOS app sends to ProAgentStore services and what it stores on your device.

Data We Collect

The app communicates with the ProAgentStore API to provide core app functionality. Depending on how you use the service, this can include:

  • Account identifiers needed to authenticate your ProAgentStore account.
  • Agent chat messages and responses.
  • Runtime task, approval, cancellation, and input events.
  • Coding repository metadata, session messages, terminal output, and activity timeline data.
  • Profile context and special instructions you save for your agents.

On-Device Storage

The iOS app stores your ProAgentStore session token in the iOS Keychain. The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, photos, precise location, or health data.

Tracking and Ads

The app does not use third-party advertising, third-party analytics, or cross-app tracking.

Third-Party Services

PAGS uses ProAgentStore services hosted at api.proagentstore.online. Agent runtimes may call tools, model providers, repositories, or other services that you configure or authorize in ProAgentStore.

Your Responsibilities

Do not submit secrets, regulated data, or sensitive personal information to an agent unless you intend that agent and its configured tools to process it.

Data Retention

Data associated with your ProAgentStore account is retained to provide the service unless deleted through account or workspace controls. Deleting the iOS app removes local app data from the device, but does not automatically delete server-side account, agent, or runtime data.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to privacy@proagentstore.online.
